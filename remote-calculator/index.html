The range comes from the sick + admin buffer: 6 days (low) to 12 days (high). As an employee, sick days were paid. As a self-employed person, they're not — and you'll spend time on invoicing, contracts, and admin that used to be invisible. The 13.8× multiplier accounts for 12 monthly salaries + holiday pay (lomakorvaus) + holiday bonus (lomaraha). Accounting costs (50 €/month) are standard for a basic bookkeeper in Finland.
