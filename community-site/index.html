A Slack full of marketers who actually talk to each other.

Someone posts a job opening, three people reply with referrals. Someone's stuck on a campaign, five people jump in with ideas. Someone lands their first marketing gig in Finland and the whole channel celebrates. That's a normal Tuesday for us.

We do events too. Mostly Helsinki, but we've been to Oulu (2026) and Tampere (2023). Afterworks, webinars on everything from SEO to salary negotiation, side events at Slush. You'll leave with new friends and real answers to stuff you've been stuck on.

Along the way, we built a freelance rate calculator, ran salary surveys, put together a first-job guide, and recorded a bunch of webinars. We keep making things because it's fun and someone out there will find it useful.

English-speaking, immigrant-friendly, zero tolerance for discrimination. No corporate agenda. Just 50+ volunteers who run this in their spare time because it makes them happy.
